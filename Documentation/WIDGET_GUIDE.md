## How to Add the Widget

### On Android 12+:
1. **Long press** on your home screen
2. Tap **"Widgets"**
3. Scroll to find **"MealTrack"**
4. **Long press** the widget and drag it to your home screen
5. **Resize** the widget by dragging the corners

### On Android 11 and below:
1. **Long press** on your home screen
2. Tap **"Widgets"**
3. Find **"MealTrack"** in the list
4. **Drag** it to your home screen
5. **Resize** as needed
